\begin{equation}
\mathbf{a}_{\rm total} = -\frac{GM}{r^2}\hat{\mathbf{r}} + W \frac{G M}{r_0 r} \hat{\mathbf{r}} \label{eq:atotal}
\end{equation}

This form ensures an asymptotically flat rotation curve ($v^2(r) \to W \frac{GM}{r_0}$ at large $r$). The transition radius where the informational term equals the Newtonian term in magnitude is:

\begin{equation}
r_{\rm trans} = \frac{r_0}{W} \approx 12.5 \, r_0 \label{eq:rtrans}
\end{equation}
